About Us

I'm Vaishali Prajapati, a 22 years old UI/UX Designer. My responsibility is to design and implementation of all the experiences a user has when interacting with a digital tool, such as a website & Mobile Application.

My job is to create easy-to-use and aesthetically pleasant interfaces by analysing the users' needs and researching the best ways to create usable and successful products. User-centered designs come from a user-centered workflow: as a UI/UX Designer I understand the importance of data analysis, research, wireframing. prototyping, and usability testing.
